About T&TG Trade Corp

A Toronto-headquartered global trade corporation founded on the vision of connecting emerging and established markets through technology, trust, and strategic investment.

Who We Are

Tom & The Group Trade Corp

Founded on October 14, 2026, T&TG Trade Corp operates a global e-commerce trading portal designed to facilitate seamless domestic and international commerce. The corporation manages subsidiaries specialising in import and export operations, enabling businesses to access products and markets across multiple regions.

In addition to trade services, the corporation provides foreign exchange solutions to support cross-border transactions and manages strategic investment activities aimed at generating long-term growth and financial stability.

Through technology-driven platforms and international partnerships, the company focuses on efficient global trade, currency management, and diversified investment opportunities.

Our Mission
